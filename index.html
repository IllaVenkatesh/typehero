About TypeHero

From Zero to Typing Hero

TypeHero is designed to help anyone learn the muscle memory required to type rapidly and accurately without looking at their hands. Touch typing lets you write thoughts smoothly, improving efficiency in programming, writing, and daily communication.

Why Practice in Levels?

By splitting practice into letters (Beginner), sentences (Intermediate), and paragraphs (Pro), you systematically train different levels of muscle memory without burning out.

The Home Row Position

Place your left fingers on A-S-D-F and right fingers on J-K-L-;. Rest your thumbs on the spacebar. Let the physical bumps on 'F' and 'J' guide you back without looking down.

Aesthetic Audio

TypeHero uses built-in audio synthesis to mimic a tactile mechanical keyboard. This helps build a sound rhythm matching your keystrokes, which improves typing cadence.

💡 Expert Typing Tips

  • Do not look down: Force yourself to look only at the screen. Beginner mode highlights keys on the virtual keyboard to help transition.
  • Prioritize Accuracy: Speed comes naturally once your fingers know where to go. Aim for 95%+ accuracy before trying to speed up.
  • Type regularly: Even 5–10 minutes of daily typing can yield major improvements in just a few weeks.
